About Mengtang Li

Mengtang Li received his B.S. degree with honor in Astronautics Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China in 2014, and the M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Vanderbilt University, Nashville, TN, USA in 2016. He is working towards the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering at Vanderbilt University and is expected to graduate in August 2020.
His research interests are various and versatile, including:

  1. high pressure fluid power system: variable displacement hydrualic pump/motor design, alternating flow application.
  2. low pressure fluid power system: compact and efficient power unit for soft robotics actuation.
  3. mecahnical circulatory support device: total artificial heart and ventricle assist device design, hemocompatibility test.
  4. wearable sensor for clinical application: potential frailty detection and assessment.
